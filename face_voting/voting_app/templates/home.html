About Online Voting

Online voting systems improve accessibility and convenience in elections by enabling voters to cast their votes remotely using secure digital platforms.

This system integrates biometric face recognition technology to verify voter identity and ensure that only registered voters can participate in the election process.

By combining modern authentication techniques with secure voting protocols, online voting systems aim to increase voter participation while maintaining transparency and integrity in the electoral process.
